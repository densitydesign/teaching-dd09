FIRST OVERVIEW
OF THE DATA

WhoSampled is a detailed database of samples, cover songs and remixes. It explores the entire history of music spanning over 1000 years and documenting musical connections.

CIRCULARITY
& CYCLICITY
IN MUSIC COVER PRODUCTION

Genres are born, then fade away and finally are taken back after some years. The data reveals the cyclicity of this fascinating phenomenon.

GENRE EVOLUTION

Rock/Pop leads cover production, being responsible for almost half of all the covers, even though it entered the music business almost late, during the 1950s. Here we explore the stake of each genre in terms of cover production per year.

COVER TRACES

In the course of the 20th century the single music genres had differing activity levels, ranging from only few years of high production to all century long activity. Here we can see the most active period for each genre.

CONNECTING DOTS

Listing the most influential titles generated by a genre in connection with the corresponding production periods. Can a genre’s cover production be linked to a specific originating decade?

TOP 10: MOST INFLUENT SONGS

“Yesterday” by The Beatles is the most covered song with 123 covers. Having its origin in Rock/Pop, it has been transformed by artists with different musical backgrounds.

FROM GENRE
TO GENRE

It is striking that the majority of originals are being covered either within the same (original) genre or by the genre Rock/Pop.
