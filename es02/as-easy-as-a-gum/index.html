The Emergency Contraception issue has always been fostered
by the unintended pregnancies rate in the USA,
the highest among developed countries.

Unintended pregnancies are pregnancies that are mistimed, unplanned or unwanted at the time of conception. Unintended pregnancy mainly results from the lack of, inconsistent, or incorrect use of effective contraceptive methods.

The U.S. unintended pregnancies rate is significantly higher
than the rate in many other developed countries.

Over-the-counter (OTC) drugs are medicines sold directly to a consumer without a prescription from a healthcare professional. In many countries, OTC drugs are selected by a regulatory agency to ensure that ingredients are safe and effective when used without a physician's care.
