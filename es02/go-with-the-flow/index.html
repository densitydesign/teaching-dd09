did you know...

There are thousands of rivers on Earth, from the largest to the smallest. But what happens when they can't keep all their water anymore?

what is a flood?

Flood is an extreme weather event that affects coasts and rivers. River flood is a continuous overflow of water from the banks of rivers.

causes

Heavy rains, hurricanes and droughts are natural causes of flooding. Also deforestation and poor farming can be responsible for floods.

human

natural

consequences

Millions of people die and billions of dollars are spent due to floods, both in rich and poor countries.

is there a solution?

River flood management assumes that it is necessary to learn to live with floods as best, reducing their speed, size and duration. There are different methods.

hard engineering

soft engineering

Hard engineering is defined as controlled disruption of natural processes by using man-made structures.

Soft engineering uses ecological principles and practices to achieve the stabilization and safety of the rivers.
